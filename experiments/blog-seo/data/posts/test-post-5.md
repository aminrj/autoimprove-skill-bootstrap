# Threat Modeling for AI Systems

As AI becomes more prevalent in applications, understanding potential threats is crucial for building secure systems.

## Common AI Threats

- **Data Poisoning**: Malicious inputs to corrupt training data
- **Model Inversion**: Extracting sensitive information from models
- **Adversarial Examples**: Inputs designed to fool AI systems

## Mitigation Strategies

- Implement adversarial training
- Use differential privacy techniques
- Regular model auditing
- Secure data governance

This post provides a foundation for threat modeling in AI systems.
